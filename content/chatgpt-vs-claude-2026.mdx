## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use only the free versions?

Yes. Many professionals can delay paying until AI becomes part of their daily workflow.

### Is Claude better than ChatGPT?

For long documents and deep reasoning, often yes. For everyday productivity, ChatGPT remains the better all-round choice.

### Is ChatGPT worth paying for?

For most professionals, yes. If AI saves you even one or two hours per month, the subscription often pays for itself.

## Final Verdict

If you're only paying for **one** AI subscription today, choose **ChatGPT Plus**.

If your job revolves around reading, analysing and summarising large amounts of information, **Claude Pro** becomes an excellent second subscription.

The goal isn't to collect AI subscriptions. It's to build an AI workflow that genuinely saves time.
