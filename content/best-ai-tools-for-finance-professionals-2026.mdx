## Where AI Actually Helps In Finance

The most realistic finance use cases are not magical stock predictions. They are much less glamorous and much more useful: understanding a formula, summarizing a report, building a checklist, comparing assumptions and creating a cleaner management note.

In my experience, the best finance AI prompts are narrow. Instead of asking "analyze this company," ask "summarize revenue drivers, margin risks and working capital changes from this text." The narrower prompt usually gives you something you can actually verify.

## Tool-By-Tool Finance Breakdown

### Claude: Best For Annual Reports And Long PDFs

Claude is useful when the source material is long and you need structure: key risks, segment performance, management commentary, accounting notes or policy summaries.

### ChatGPT: Best For Excel Logic And Scenarios

ChatGPT is useful for explaining formulas, building scenario lists, debugging spreadsheet logic and turning rough notes into a memo outline.

### Perplexity: Best For Market Context

Perplexity is useful at the start of research, when you need sources and a map of what to read next. Do not treat it as the final answer.

### Grammarly: Best For Client-Ready Polish

Finance writing often needs to be clear, careful and boring in the right way. Grammarly helps remove avoidable mistakes before a note goes out.

| Model check | Explain what this formula is doing and what assumptions it depends on |

| Risk review | List the top risks and what data I should verify before concluding |

## What AI Should Not Do In Finance

- Make final investment recommendations without review
- Replace source documents
- Handle confidential client data without permission
- Invent assumptions to fill missing data
- Give tax, legal or regulated advice as a final answer

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best AI tool for finance professionals?

Claude is strong for long reports, while ChatGPT is more flexible for formulas, explanations, summaries and workflow design.

### Can AI do financial analysis?

AI can assist with analysis, but it should not be treated as the final authority. Calculations, assumptions and sources must be checked.

### Is ChatGPT good for Excel?

Yes. ChatGPT is useful for explaining formulas, building logic, debugging spreadsheet errors and creating analysis frameworks.

### Which AI tool is best for market research?

Perplexity is useful for source-backed research discovery, but finance users should verify important claims from primary sources.

### Can AI replace a finance analyst?

No. AI can speed up reading, drafting and checking, but finance work still needs source review, assumptions, controls and accountability.

### Is it safe to upload financial documents to AI tools?

Only if your employer, client and tool settings allow it. When in doubt, do not upload confidential or regulated documents.
