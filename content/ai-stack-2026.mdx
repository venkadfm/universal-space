## Before You Spend Money

Most professionals make the same mistake: they subscribe to too many AI tools before knowing how those tools fit into their workflow.

A better approach is simple:

- Start free.
- Build a daily AI habit.
- Upgrade only when a tool saves real time.
- Add specialist tools only when they solve a specific problem.

<WarningBox title="Avoid This Mistake">
Do not buy an AI tool just because it is popular. Buy it only when it solves a recurring problem in your work.
</WarningBox>

## The Tools

### ChatGPT

**Purpose:** Your all-round AI assistant.

ChatGPT is useful because it handles many different tasks well: emails, presentations, brainstorming, learning, spreadsheets, coding and general problem-solving.

| Metric | Score |
|---|---:|
| Time saved | 10/10 |
| Ease of use | 10/10 |
| Value for professionals | 9/10 |
| Best fit | General productivity |

**Buy if:**

- You use AI every day.
- You want one tool for many tasks.
- You need help with writing, planning, coding or analysis.

**Don't buy if:**

- You only use AI occasionally.
- The free version already solves your use case.

<ProTip title="How to Use ChatGPT Better">
Don't ask only simple questions. Give ChatGPT context, role, goal and output format. Better prompts produce much better answers.
</ProTip>

### Claude

**Purpose:** Deep thinking, long documents and connected workflows.

Claude is especially strong when your work involves large amounts of information: reports, policies, contracts, transcripts, research papers and strategic documents.

| Metric | Score |
|---|---:|
| Long documents | 10/10 |
| Reasoning | 10/10 |
| Writing clarity | 9/10 |
| Best fit | Deep work and analysis |

**Buy if:**

- You regularly analyse reports or PDFs.
- Your work involves long documents.
- You need thoughtful, structured answers.

**Don't buy if:**

- You only ask quick questions.
- You don't work with large information-heavy tasks.

<RecommendationBox title="When Claude May Be Better Than ChatGPT">
If your work involves long reports, document review or deep reasoning, Claude may be a better first paid subscription than ChatGPT.
</RecommendationBox>

### Perplexity

**Purpose:** Research with sources.

Perplexity is useful when you need quick research, fact-checking and source-backed answers. It is especially helpful when you don't want to rely only on an AI model's memory.

| Metric | Score |
|---|---:|
| Research | 10/10 |
| Source citations | 10/10 |
| Writing | 7/10 |
| Best fit | Research and verification |

**Buy if:**

- Research is part of your daily job.
- You regularly compare products, companies or industries.
- You need sources.

**Don't buy if:**

- You mostly need writing or brainstorming.

### Gemini

**Purpose:** Google Workspace productivity.

Gemini is useful if your work already happens inside Google's ecosystem, including Gmail, Docs, Sheets and Drive.

**Buy if:**

- You use Google Workspace heavily.
- You want AI assistance inside Gmail, Docs and Sheets.

**Don't buy if:**

- You don't use Google's ecosystem much.

### Cursor

**Purpose:** AI-assisted coding.

Cursor is built for developers. It helps write code, understand projects, refactor files and speed up software development.

**Buy if:**

- You code regularly.
- You are building apps, websites or tools.
- You want AI inside your editor.

**Don't buy if:**

- You don't write or maintain code.

## Before You Upgrade

Ask yourself:

- Will this save me at least 3–5 hours every month?
- Will I use it every week?
- Does it solve a problem my current tools don't?

If the answer is no, stay on the free version.

<WarningBox title="Do Not Confuse Popular With Useful">
The best AI tool is not the one with the most hype. It is the one that fits your workflow and saves measurable time.
</WarningBox>

## Final Recommendation

If you are new to AI, start with the free stack:

- ChatGPT Free
- Perplexity Free

If AI becomes part of your daily workflow, upgrade one tool at a time.

The goal is not to collect subscriptions. The goal is to build a simple AI stack that helps you work smarter.
