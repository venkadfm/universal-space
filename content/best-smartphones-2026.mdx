## Buying Guide

### Buy an iPhone if...

You want long software support, strong resale value, reliable performance and a simple ecosystem that works well with MacBook, iPad and Apple Watch.

### Buy a Pixel if...

You want the strongest AI features, clean Android and excellent Google integration.

### Buy a Samsung Ultra if...

You want a large display, S Pen, premium hardware and productivity features.

### Buy OnePlus if...

You want strong performance, battery life and fast charging at a better value than ultra-premium flagships.

### Buy Xiaomi Ultra if...

Your main priority is camera hardware and photography.

<ProTip title="Universal Space Buying Tip">
If you already use a MacBook, the iPhone is usually the smoother professional choice. If you live inside Google apps, Pixel becomes more attractive.
</ProTip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which phone is best for AI features?

The Google Pixel 10 Pro is the strongest AI-focused choice because Google's AI features are deeply integrated into the phone experience.

### Which phone should most professionals buy?

Most professionals should consider the iPhone 17 because it offers strong performance, reliable software, long-term support and high resale value.

### Which Android phone is best?

The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is the best premium Android phone for power users, while the Pixel 10 is better value for most people.

### Which phone has the best battery?

The OnePlus 15 is the best choice if battery life and fast charging are your top priorities.

### Should I buy the most expensive phone?

Not always. Buy the phone that fits your use case. A professional who mainly uses email, calls, AI apps and browsing may not need the most expensive flagship.
